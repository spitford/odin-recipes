Butter-Roasted Cauliflower

Description

The second best dish to pair with lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 head Cauliflower, cut into large florets
  • 2 tablespoons Butter, melted, or more to taste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Spread cauliflower onto baking sheet
  3. Roast in the oven for 30 mins.
  4. Flip and continue baking for 10 minutes more.
