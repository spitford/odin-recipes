Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, possibly the best in the world!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instrunctions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
